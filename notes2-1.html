Unit 2.1 Derivatives

MODULE: CORE DEFINITION & RATE OF CHANGE
01 Core Definition of a Derivative
The derivative represents the rate of change of a function with respect to a variable. It is fundamentally a function itself, where the independent variable is usually the input \(x\) and the dependent variable is the Instantaneous Rate of Change (IROC). 导数表示函数相对于变量的变化率。它本质上本身也是一个函数，其中自变量通常是输入值 \(x\)，而因变量则是该点的瞬时变化率 (IROC)。
Graphical Interpretation 几何意义
  • >> The value of the derivative at a point is equal to the slope of the tangent line to the curve at that point.
  • >> 函数在某一点处的导数值等于该曲线在该点处的切线斜率。
  • >> Denotation (记法): \(f'(x)\)
02 AROC vs. IROC & The Limit Definition
Average Rate of Change (AROC) is the slope of the secant line between two points. To find the Instantaneous Rate of Change (IROC), we take the limit of the AROC as the distance between the two points approaches zero. 平均变化率 (AROC) 是连接两点之割线的斜率。而要求解瞬时变化率 (IROC)，我们则需要计算当两点间水平距离无限接近于零时 AROC 的极限。
\[AROC = \frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x} = \frac{f(x_2) - f(x_1)}{x_2 - x_1}\]
01 // GENERAL LIMIT DEFINITION (ANY \(x\))
\[f'(x) = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x+h) - f(x)}{h}\]
Squeezing horizontal distance \(h\) between two points. 挤压两点间的水平距离 \(h \to 0\)，使之融合成一点。
02 // POINT-SPECIFIC DEFINITION (\(x = c\))
\[f'(c) = \lim_{x \to c} \frac{f(x) - f(c)}{x - c}\]
Moving a dynamic point \(x\) closer and closer to a fixed target \(c\). 将动态坐标点 \(x\) 无限推向固定目标坐标点 \(c\)。
03 Graphical Failure of Differentiability
The derivative fails to exist (DNE) at points where the slope is not uniquely or infinitely defined. Below are the standard non-differentiable scenarios. 在斜率未被唯一确定或趋于无穷的坐标点，导数不存在 (DNE)。以下是微积分中三种最典型不可导的几何场景。
01 // FROM SECANT TO TANGENT
Secant (Orange dashed) approaches Tangent (Cyan line) as \(h \to 0\). 当 \(h \to 0\) 时，割线（橙色虚线）逼近切线（青色实线）。
02 // SHARP CORNER (CUSP)
Slope changes abruptly. Left slope \(\neq\) Right slope: \(f'(c^-) \neq f'(c^+)\). 斜率突变。左右两侧极限导数不相等，如 \(y = |x|\)。
03 // VERTICAL TANGENT
Slope becomes infinite. \(\lim_{x \to c} f'(x) = \pm\infty\), as in \(y = \sqrt[3]{x}\). 切线斜率垂直趋近于无穷。导数为无穷大，无法定量定义。
04 Differentiability & Continuity Rules

One-Sided Derivatives

Left-Hand Derivative \(f'(c^-)\): The limit slope as we approach \(c\) from the left (\(x \to c^-\)). 左侧导数 \(f'(c^-)\)： 自左侧逼近 \(c\) 点时的极限斜率。
Right-Hand Derivative \(f'(c^+)\): The limit slope as we approach \(c\) from the right (\(x \to c^+\)). 右侧导数 \(f'(c^+)\)： 自右侧逼近 \(c\) 点时的极限斜率。
The Requirement for Differentiability 可导性的充要条件
A function \(f\) is differentiable at \(c\) if and only if: 函数 \(f\) 在 \(c\) 点处可导，当且仅当满足以下两个核心逻辑：
  • 1. \(f\) is continuous at \(c\). (函数在 \(c\) 点处连续)
  • 2. Left-hand derivative equals right-hand derivative: \(f'(c^-) = f'(c^+)\). (左右两侧极限斜率相等)

Crucial Logical Distinction

Differentiability \(\implies\) Continuity
If a function is differentiable at a point, it is guaranteed to be continuous at that point. 可导必连续：如果函数在某点可导，则其在该点必然连续。
Continuity \(\centernot\implies\) Differentiability
A function can be continuous but still fail to be differentiable (e.g., at a sharp corner or cusp). 连续不一定可导：即使函数在该点连续，也有可能不可导（例如尖角处）。
05 Worked Examples

Example 01: Limit Definition Evaluation

Use the limit definition to find the derivative of \( f(x) = x^2 \) at \( x = 3 \). 使用导数的极限定义求出 \( f(x) = x^2 \) 在 \( x = 3 \) 处的导数。
SYSTEM LOG: Evaluated using Point-Specific Limit Formula
Using the definition: \(f'(c) = \lim_{x \to c} \frac{f(x) - f(c)}{x - c}\) with \(c = 3\) and \(f(x) = x^2\). 套用特定点极限定义公式：
\[f'(3) = \lim_{x \to 3} \frac{x^2 - 3^2}{x - 3}\] \[f'(3) = \lim_{x \to 3} \frac{(x-3)(x+3)}{x - 3} \quad \text{[Factor the difference of squares]}\] \[f'(3) = \lim_{x \to 3} (x + 3) \quad \text{[Cancel the shared factor (x-3)]}\] \[f'(3) = 3 + 3 = \mathbf{6}\]

Example 02: Differentiability of Piecewise Function

Determine if the piecewise function \( f(x) = \begin{cases} x^2 & x \le 1 \\ 2x - 1 & x > 1 \end{cases} \) is differentiable at \( x = 1 \). 判定分段函数 \( f(x) \) 在分界点 \( x = 1 \) 处是否可导。
SYSTEM LOG: Two-Step Verification Protocol
Step 1: Check Continuity at \(x = 1\)
Left Limit: \(\lim_{x \to 1^-} f(x) = 1^2 = 1\)
Right Limit: \(\lim_{x \to 1^+} f(x) = 2(1) - 1 = 1\)
Value: \(f(1) = 1\). Thus, \(f(x)\) is continuous. 左极限为 1，右极限为 1，函数值也为 1。由此证明：函数在该点连续。
Step 2: Compare Left and Right Derivatives
Left-Hand Derivative: \(f'(1^-) = \lim_{x \to 1^-} \frac{x^2 - 1}{x - 1} = \lim_{x \to 1^-} (x + 1) = \mathbf{2}\) 左导数极限值为 2。
Right-Hand Derivative: \(f'(1^+) = \lim_{x \to 1^+} \frac{(2x - 1) - 1}{x - 1} = \lim_{x \to 1^+} \frac{2(x-1)}{x-1} = \mathbf{2}\) 右导数极限值亦为 2。
>> RESULT: Since Left Derivative (2) = Right Derivative (2), and the function is continuous, f(x) IS differentiable at x = 1 with f'(1) = 2.
>> 结论：左右导数极限相等，且函数连续，故在该分界点处可导。
