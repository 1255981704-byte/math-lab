01 The Area Problem
In geometry, calculating the area of regular shapes like rectangles is easy. But how do we find the exact area under a curved graph? 在几何中，计算规则图形（如矩形）的面积很容易。但在微积分中，我们如何求出一条曲线下方的精确面积？
STRATEGY: Approximation 策略：逼近法
  • >> Slice the area into thin vertical strips. (切割成垂直条)
  • >> Treat each strip as a rectangle. (视为矩形)
  • >> Add up their areas. (求和)
02 Notation & Syntax

Sigma Notation (\(\Sigma\))

\[\sum_{k=1}^{n} a_k = a_1 + a_2 + a_3 + \cdots + a_n\]

The Riemann Sum Definition

Given a function \( f(x) \) on interval \([a, b]\) partitioned into \( n \) subintervals: 给定函数 \( f(x) \) 在区间 \([a, b]\) 上，被分割为 \( n \) 个子区间:
WIDTH (\(\Delta x\))
\[\Delta x = \frac{b - a}{n}\]
HEIGHT (\(f(x^*)\))
Value of function at sample point.
(由采样点决定的高度)
03 Approximation Methods
Visualizing the 4 standard methods on \(f(x) = 0.5x^2 + 1\). 可视化 4 种标准逼近方法。
LRAM (Left)
Use Left Endpoint. Underestimate for Increasing func. 左端点。递增函数时为低估。
RRAM (Right)
Use Right Endpoint. Overestimate for Increasing func. 右端点。递增函数时为高估。
MRAM (Midpoint)
Use Midpoint. Generally more accurate. 中点。通常更精确。
TRAPEZOIDAL
Connect points linearly. Average of LRAM/RRAM. 梯形连线。LRAM与RRAM的平均值。
04 Worked Examples

Example 01: Polynomial Calculation

Calculate Left Riemann Sum for \( f(x) = x^2 + 1 \) on \([0, 2]\), \( n = 4 \). 计算 \( f(x) = x^2 + 1 \) 在 \([0, 2]\) 上的左黎曼和 (\(n=4\))。
SYSTEM LOG:
1. Width: \(\Delta x = (2-0)/4 = 0.5\)
2. Points (Left): \(0, 0.5, 1.0, 1.5\)
3. Heights: \(f(0)=1, f(0.5)=1.25, f(1)=2, f(1.5)=3.25\)
Sum = \(0.5 \cdot (1 + 1.25 + 2 + 3.25)\)
Sum = \(0.5 \cdot (7.5) = \mathbf{3.75}\)
